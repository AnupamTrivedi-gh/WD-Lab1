Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a key role in India's missile and nuclear programs. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he inspired millions with his vision and simplicity.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
  • Served as President of India (2002–2007)

Inspiration

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action." — Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam
